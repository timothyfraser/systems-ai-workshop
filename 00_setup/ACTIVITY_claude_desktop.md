# 🔧 SETUP · Claude Desktop

> ⏱️ ~10 min. Anthropic's desktop app for **Claude** — conversational analysis, Skills, and the home of **Claude Code**.

1. Install from [claude.ai/download](https://claude.ai/download) (macOS/Windows) and open it.
2. Sign in. **Cornell note:** if your department has a Claude for Work/Team plan, sign in with your Cornell email; otherwise a free or Pro account is fine.
3. Confirm it opens and a new chat replies to "Hello". (We'll open this repo's [`.claude/`](../.claude/) Skills together on the day.)

> A **Skill** is a small `SKILL.md` folder that teaches Claude one job well — like building a poster or red-teaming a paper. This repo ships several; you don't need to write any.
